Cornbread

Description

What? You're just gonna leave all that delicious sauce on the plate? Nope, take some nice, cakey cornbread to polish it all up

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
